project leafie

What is LEAFIE?

Intelligent plant that tweets to you

LEAFIE is a smart plant that utilizes twitter to send tweets to the user, prompting them to water LEAFIE when it is feeling thirsty.

LEAFIE takes a selfie when watered

Whenever the user waters LEAFIE, it will detect the level of moisture. If the moisture is sufficient, LEAFIE will take a selfie to tweet its photo and post a message of gratitude to the user.

A plant with personality

The photo selfies and messages which LEAFIE tweets are based on its personality. This provides a better experience and interaction to the user, as it becomes more of a human behaviour that makes the user treat it like a living thing.

List of Design Requirements

1. Moisture Detection- ability to sense presence/lack of water in soil.

2. Wireless Internet Connection- ability to be connected to the internet and its own Twitter profile.

3. Self tweeting- ability to tweet its soil’s status of freshly-watered.

4. Selfie photography- ability to take photo of itself when watered.

5. Photo hosting- ability to upload photo of itself to its Twitter profile.

6. Data in array- ability to tweet different phrases with photo to avoid repetition.

7. Twitter tag- ability to tag other people/plants in its tweets.

8. Self response- ability to “comment” on other tweets with responses from a database of phrases.

9. Celebrations- ability to acknowledge holidays, birthdays, and other special occasions.

Structure Diagram

Process communication of Leafie

The LEAFIE components are connected to the arduino, where it can send data back and forth to PubNub. The PubNub will act as a socket to process data into the website's database. The web database will pull the data to the twitter account where the target audience receives the data. While, the LEAFIE camera will receive data from the web database where it will post images to twitter.

Components & Mockups

Webcam

It will be placed beside the plant to ensure photo is taken during an event of the plant being watered. The web cam will be connected to a laptop to send data to the arduino.

Moisture Sensor

The Moisture Sensor is placed on the soil to detect the moisture level of the plant. Whenever the sensor detects insufficient water, it will prompt LEAFIE to send data to twitter, prompting user to water it. Likewise, when the plant is watered, it will send gratitude to the user.

Arduino

We will be using arduino as a platform to integrate the functions of the plant sending and receiving data from the twitter account.

Wi-fi/ Web

Everything comes together on a simple website, which translate the information about the soil, the captured photo, and a pre-written text into a tweet, which then is uploaded to LEAFIE's Twitter account.

How it Works?

A step by step detailed walkthrough.

1. Contant Moisture Sensor Detection

The moisture sensor which is placed in the user's plant, will constantly monitor and detect whether the plant's soil is moist or dry.

2. The Plant Calls For Help

If the sensor detects dry soil in the user's plant, it will send its data to the web database. There will be an array of sentences which the plant will tweet, but it will randomize one sentence to prompt the user to water it.

3. User's twitter receives notification from plant's account

The plant will tweet a random sentence to the user's twitter account, reminding the user to water it before it drys out.

4. User acknowledges and waters the plant

Whenever the the user acknowledges the notification and waters the plant, the moisture sensor will do the same process in step 1. If the plant received enough moisture, it will take a selfie and tweet a photo with gratitude and thank the user.
