Introducing Leafie

A new companion for you

We have developed a new smart plant kit which interacts with people. It is packed with loads of features such as tweeting, selfies and it has a personality!


A Plant that tweets to you

Leafie is a smart plant that is able to tweet to you when its thirsty. It acts as a great reminder to the users, so that they do not forget to water their plants.


A Plant that takes selfies

Whenever Leafie is watered, it will automatically take a selfie by itself and post a tweet of appreciation with photo.
